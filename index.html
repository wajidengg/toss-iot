Welcome to the Smart Systems and IoT Club

About Us

The Smart Systems and IoT Club at Northeastern University is dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and learning in the fields of IoT and smart systems. Join us to explore cutting-edge technology and build your skills in an engaging and supportive community.

Mission Statement

Our Mission

We aim to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and practical applications by hosting workshops, hackathons, guest lectures, and collaborative projects.

Leadership Roles

  • President: Oversees all activities and represents the club.
  • Vice President: Assists the president and manages initiatives.
  • Treasurer: Handles club finances and budgeting.
  • Secretary: Maintains records and manages internal communication.
  • Events Coordinator: Plans and executes events.
  • Marketing & Communications Manager: Promotes the club and manages social media.
  • Technical Lead: Guides technical projects and workshops.
  • Membership Coordinator: Focuses on recruitment and member engagement.
